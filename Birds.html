PARROT

Parrots are known for their intelligence, unique personalities, long lifespan (in some species, over 30 years!) and striking colouring. Some varieties, such as African Greys, Amazons and Quakers have tremendous vocal ability, resulting in the capacity to mimic the human voice, much to their owner’s delight.

TOUCAN
